Sync-jack pins unconfirmed

The skill corpus lists the second-jack / MIDI GPIOs as a known-unknown — not in the published pinout. The tape-looper firmware (June 2026) drives them, citing "TimK's sync-jack schematic." Recorded here as a candidate resolution to verify against TimK, not as established fact:

PinFunctionSignal
P0.23MIDI out (BC807 PNP base → SYNC_RING; PNP inverts)31250-baud, 24-PPQN clock + Start/Stop
P0.20Pocket-Operator sync (PO_A → SYNC_TIP)~5 ms pulse, 2 PPQN
P0.17Pocket-Operator sync (PO_B → SYNC_TIP, paralleled)as above
✕ Don't restate as fact yet

This contradicts the earlier "MIDI sync rides the TRS jack by a path not in the published pinout" status. Both the looper's app.overlay (which avoids P1.04 as the BT UART) and its MIDI code are one author's reading. Confirm with TimK before treating P0.23/P0.20/P0.17 as canonical.
